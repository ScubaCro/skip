How to buy

Create a Wallet

Download a software wallet that is compatible with Cronos such as the DefiWallet or Metamask. You can also tie this to your favorite hardware wallet for extra security!

Get Some CRO

If you do not already have some CRO on Cronos you can use a service such as XY Finance to bridge from ETH or other native chains to CRO on Cronos.

Go to VVS Finance

Go to vvs.finance and use the swap feature on the homepage. Connect your wallet and confirm you can see your CRO in your wallet. Next take the contract address adove and add $SKIP to your token list.

Swap CRO for $SKIP

Swap CRO for $SKIP. $SKIP has ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
